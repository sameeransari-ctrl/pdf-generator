## 💡 How to Use

### For Safety Valve Reports

1. **Open** `safety_valve_form.html`
2. Fill in **Client Information**
3. Fill in **Valve Identification**
4. Enter **Set Pressure** → watch tolerances calculate! ⚡
5. Fill in **Gauge Details**
6. Enter **Test Results**
7. Add **Comments**
8. Click **"Generate PDF Report"**

### For Pressure Vessel Reports

1. **Open** `pressure_vessel_form.html`
2. Fill in **Client & Equipment Details**
3. Fill in **Vessel Details**
4. Fill in **Design Information**
5. Select **Inspection Results** (Pass/Fail checkboxes)
6. Add **Comments & Recommendations**
7. Click **"Generate PDF Report"**

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

### Form won't open?
- Right-click → "Open with" → Choose your browser

### Calculations not working?
- Make sure you're typing numbers only
- Check the blue highlighted "Set Pressure" field
- Refresh browser (F5)

### Can't type in gray fields?
- This is correct! Gray fields are auto-calculated (read-only)
- You only enter the set pressure

### Need more help?
- See **USER_GUIDE.md** for detailed instructions
- Contact your system administrator
